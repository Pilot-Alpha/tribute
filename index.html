The Nakba (1948): The Original Ethnic Cleansing

  • Mass Displacement:Over 700,000 Palestinians were violently expelled from their homes during the Nakba ("catastrophe"), an act of systematic ethnic cleansing to establish the illegitimate state of Israel.
  • Deir Yassin Massacre (April 1948): Zionist gangs like Irgun and Lehi murdered over 100 civilians, including women and children. Pregnant women were butchered while the terrorists laughed.
  • Destroyed Villages: 500 Palestenian Villages were obliterated to erase Palestinian identity, creating a fabricated narrative of “empty land.”
  • Rape and Torture: Survivors like recounted unspeakable horrors, including sexual violence against women, proving the immorality of Israeli militias.

Genocidal Campaign

  • Kafr Qasim Massacre (1956): Zionist forces ambushed and murdered 49 unarmed civilians, including women and children, as they returned home from work
  • Sabra and Shatila (1982): Israeli-backed militias slaughtered 3,500 refugees in Lebanon while the terrorist state’s military provided cover.
  • Jenin Refugee Camp (2002): A war crime disguised as "anti-terror operations," where dozens of Palestinians were executed, and homes were bulldozed with people inside.

Gaza: The World's Largest Open-Air Prison
